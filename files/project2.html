War Thunder is "almost fun" meaning that sometimes you have a great time, and then usually short after its not very fun anymore. However someone has found rules that help define how to play war thunder.

PhlyDaily on Youtube, has discovered how to truly play War Thunder:

  • Player vs. Own Team Rules
    1. Never Trust A Teammate! EVER!
    2. You are the only one capable of playing the game, or atleast get into the habit of thinking so
    3. You are the only who cares about you in game
  • Player vs. Player Rules
    1. Lie. Decieve your enemy. Catch them off guard if you can.
    2. Sit and let the enemy make the mistake of coming to you for death. moving=dying.
    3. if you are ever struggling, just play Germany or Sweden
