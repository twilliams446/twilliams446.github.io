What to do with your parts

Now that you have all of the necessary parts, assembly is easy!

  1. first, get everything out of the box
  2. next, assemble motherboard
  3. After that, put motherboard and all inside of case
  4. now plug in anything you may not have prior to installment into case
  5. connect all wires
  6. try to turn on and watch your creation come to life
